# wind-farm-analysis-and-assessment
Comprehensive engineering study of wind farm performance, including power assessment, wake effect modelling, wind curtailment, capacity credit analysis, financial evaluation and wind measurements..

## Overview

This repository contains my engineering study on wind farm analysis and assessment. The project evaluates wind resources, turbine performance, energy production, and the technical and economic aspects of wind farm development using engineering analysis and assessment methodologies.

## Objectives

- Assess wind resources for wind farm development
- Evaluate wind farm energy production
- Analyze turbine performance and wake effects
- Assess wind curtailment and capacity credit
- Perform financial evaluation of wind energy projects

## Work Completed

### Wind Measurements
- Collection and analysis of wind data
- Wind speed and wind direction assessment
- Site characterization

### Wind Rose Analysis
- Analysis of prevailing wind directions
- Wind frequency distribution
- Wind resource visualization

### Power Assessment
- Wind power estimation
- Energy production assessment
- Turbine performance evaluation

### Wake Effect Modelling
- Analysis of turbine wake interactions
- Impact of wake losses on energy production
- Wind farm layout considerations

### Wind Curtailment
- Assessment of curtailment causes
- Impact on energy generation
- Operational considerations

### Wind Capacity Credit
- Evaluation of wind power contribution to grid reliability
- Capacity value assessment

### Financial Evaluation
- Levelized Cost of Energy (LCoE) analysis
- Economic feasibility assessment
- Cost and performance evaluation
